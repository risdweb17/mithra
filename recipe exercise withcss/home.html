Yummy in my Tummy Cheesecake /h1>

Ingredients

Tarte Portion
  • butter - 60g
  • egg yolk - 1
  • sugar - 40g
  • flour - 120g
Filling
  • heavy cream - 1 cup
  • eggs - 2
  • cream cheese - 1 pack
  • flour - 3 TBSP
  • sugar - 2/3 cup
  • lemon juice - 1 tbsp

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F
  2. Put Tartion Portion ingredientes in food processor
  3. In a cake pan, press the mix to create a crust
  4. Bake at 350 F for 10 min
  5. Put Filling Ingredients in food processor
  6. Pour onto crust
  7. Bake at 350 F for about 30 min.
    Time will depend on oven strength.
